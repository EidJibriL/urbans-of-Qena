Urban developments in Qena
(1980-2023)




The location of the study area : Qena governorate is located within the South Upper Egypt region, where it is bordered by the New Valley governorate from the west, the Red Sea governorate from the east, the Sohag governorate from the north, and the Luxor governorate from the south.







The astronomical location Qena governorate :is located between the latitudes (22 and 26.12) north, and the longitudes (32 and 34) east.
The area: The area of Qena governorate is about 13,001 km2, while the inhabited area is 10 km2
